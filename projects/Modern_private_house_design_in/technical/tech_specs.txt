**Technical Specification for Modern Private House Design in Jurmala Bulduri, Latvia**

**1. Conceptual Design and Feasibility Study**

- **Site Analysis Tools:**
  - GIS Software: ArcGIS, QGIS for topography and soil analysis.
  - Drone Imaging: DJI Drones for aerial site mapping and vegetation analysis.

- **Design Tools:**
  - Sketching Software: SketchUp for initial design concepts.
  - CAD Software: AutoCAD for detailed design sketches.

- **Regulatory Compliance:**
  - Building Information Modeling (BIM): Revit for compliance evaluation with local building codes.
  - Environmental Analysis: Ladybug Tools for climate and environmental considerations.

**2. Schematic Design and Approval**

- **Design Tools:**
  - 3D Rendering Software: Lumion or V-Ray for creating detailed visualizations.
  - Material Selection Tools: EcoSpecifier for sustainable materials selection.

- **Approval Process:**
  - Document Management: Bluebeam Revu for submission and feedback management.
  - Local Authority Integration: API integration for submission to local planning authorities if available.

**3. Design Development and Detailed Planning**

- **Architectural and Engineering Tools:**
  - BIM Software: ArchiCAD for comprehensive architectural and engineering integration.
  - MEP Design Software: Autodesk MEP for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing design.
  
- **Landscape Design:**
  - Landscape Design Software: Vectorworks Landmark for integration with natural surroundings.

**4. Construction Documentation and Tendering**

- **Documentation Tools:**
  - Construction Documentation: PlanGrid or Procore for document compilation and management.
  - Tender Management: Tenderfield for issuing and managing tender documents.

- **Contractor Selection:**
  - Bid Management Software: BidClerk or BuildingConnected for reviewing and negotiating contractor bids.

**5. Construction and Quality Assurance**

- **Construction Management:**
  - Project Management Software: Primavera P6 or Microsoft Project for construction scheduling and tracking.
  - Quality Assurance: BIM 360 for ensuring adherence to design and quality standards.

- **Sustainable Construction:**
  - Energy Analysis Tools: Sefaira for monitoring energy efficiency during construction.

**6. Completion and Handover**

- **Inspection and Handover:**
  - Inspection Tools: SnagR for final inspections and defect management.
  - Handover Documentation: Homeowner Manuals generated through software like Buildium.

- **Post-Handover Support:**
  - Feedback and Support Tools: SurveyMonkey for gathering client feedback and managing post-handover support.

By utilizing these technologies and APIs, the project can effectively translate the architect's vision into a technologically advanced and environmentally integrated modern private house design in Jurmala Bulduri, Latvia.
