**Project Implementation Plan for Modern Private House Design in Jurmala Bulduri, Latvia**

**Stage 1: Conceptual Design and Feasibility Study**  
- **Objective**: Develop initial design concepts and assess feasibility  
- **Activities**:  
  - Conduct site analysis to understand topography, soil conditions, and existing vegetation  
  - Meet with client to understand requirements, preferences, and budget  
  - Develop initial sketches and design concepts focusing on contemporary architecture, large windows, and integration with the landscape  
  - Evaluate design for compliance with local building regulations and environmental considerations  
  - Prepare feasibility report including cost estimates and timelines  

**Stage 2: Schematic Design and Approval**  
- **Objective**: Refine design concepts and obtain necessary approvals  
- **Activities**:  
  - Develop detailed floor plans, elevations, and 3D renderings  
  - Select natural materials that complement the pine forest setting  
  - Design the layout to ensure a minimum of 250 sqm living space with optimal use of the 1000 sqm plot  
  - Incorporate sustainable design features such as energy-efficient systems and materials  
  - Submit design to local authorities for approval and address any feedback or required modifications  

**Stage 3: Design Development and Detailed Planning**  
- **Objective**: Finalize design details and prepare for construction  
- **Activities**:  
  - Finalize architectural drawings, structural engineering plans, and MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing) systems  
  - Coordinate with specialists for landscape design to ensure seamless integration with the pine forest  
  - Select fixtures, finishes, and other interior elements in line with the contemporary style  
  - Prepare detailed cost estimates and adjust the design as necessary to meet budget constraints  
  - Develop a project schedule and timeline for construction phases  

**Stage 4: Construction Documentation and Tendering**  
- **Objective**: Prepare documents for construction and select contractors  
- **Activities**:  
  - Compile comprehensive construction documents including specifications and contracts  
  - Issue tender documents to select qualified contractors and suppliers  
  - Review bids and negotiate terms with potential contractors  
  - Finalize contracts with selected construction team and suppliers  
  - Obtain final permits and approvals required to commence construction  

**Stage 5: Construction and Quality Assurance**  
- **Objective**: Build the house ensuring quality and adherence to design  
- **Activities**:  
  - Begin site preparation and foundation work  
  - Monitor construction progress to ensure adherence to design, timeline, and quality standards  
  - Conduct regular site visits and meetings with contractors to address issues and ensure smooth progress  
  - Implement quality assurance and control measures throughout the construction process  
  - Ensure integration of sustainable features and adaptation to the natural landscape  

**Stage 6: Completion and Handover**  
- **Objective**: Complete construction and handover the finished house to the client  
- **Activities**:  
  - Conduct final inspections to ensure compliance with design and quality standards  
  - Rectify any defects or issues identified during inspections  
  - Finalize landscaping and ensure harmonious integration with the pine forest environment  
  - Handover the completed house to the client with all necessary documentation, warranties, and maintenance guides  
  - Gather client feedback and provide post-handover support as needed  

By following these stages, the project aims to deliver a modern private house that harmoniously blends contemporary architecture with the natural beauty of the Jurmala Bulduri landscape.
